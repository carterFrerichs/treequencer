treequencer

treequencer is a plant-like & self-similar geometry generator based on the graphical interpretation of character strings.

A Lindenmayer system (L-system) is a type of formal grammar, consisting of (1) an alphabet, or set of characters, (2) a collection of production rules that are used to derive increasingly long strings from an initial (3) axiom, and (4) a mechanism for interpreting the resulting character string as instructions for the generation of geometric structures.

treequencer was made with p5 and is indebted to the work documented in The Algorithmic Beauty of Plants, written by Przemyslaw Prusinkiewicz and Aristid Lindenmayer.
